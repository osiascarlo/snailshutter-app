## 🎨 Design Philosophy

The SnailShutter brand represents:
- **Patience**: Like a snail, good photography takes time
- **Mindfulness**: Thoughtful composition and timing
- **Nature**: Green palette connects to natural photography
- **Craft**: Combined with camera aperture for technical skill
